Issue Now

Who we are

Issue Now is an in-school organsation dedicated to raising money for good causes. Each year we get together to organise and run fundraisers for a variety of charities. We do bakesales, bucket-shaking and collect food for foodbanks as well as many other things. Run by S6s, we welcome anyone who would like to join and we meet at Monday lunchtime in M1.33.

Upcoming Events and Notices

Unfortunately, due to COVID-19, meetings of IssueNow have been suspended. This also means that many popular ways of fundraising are impossible, but we would like to encourage you to donate to food banks and charities as well as to be kind.

Get Involved

We're always looking for new participants to help us raise money for good causes. Please feel free to drop in to make suggestions or ask questions.

Meetings happen in Mr Crosbie's room (M1.33) at Monday lunchtimes.

We are also happy to support anyone wanting to run their own fundraiser for their choice charity, just come and chat to us.

S5s are especially welcome, as one or a few are needed each year to run it when they are in S6.
